# GTKY — Feature Ideas

## Gamification
- Points/XP system for answering questions and acing quizzes
- Badges and achievements (Quiz Master, Social Butterfly, Perfect Score, etc.)
- Daily streak — reward users who engage every day
- Leaderboard — who knows the group best overall

## Quiz Enhancements
- Timer mode — adds pressure and excitement
- Streak counter for consecutive correct answers during a session
- "Who knows you best?" stat — see which user has scored highest quizzing about you
- Quiz history — review past sessions and scores
- Head-to-head mode — two people quiz each other simultaneously

## Profile & Social
- "About Me" card — a shareable summary of your own answers (top fun facts)
- Compatibility percentage between two specific users
- "You both chose..." — show shared answers between you and another person
- Profile completeness bar — nudges users to answer more questions

## Discovery
- Icebreaker of the Day — a featured question shown on the home screen
- "Get to know [Name]" — tap a user and see a mini profile of their answers
- Activity feed — subtle ticker showing recent app activity ("Alex just answered 12 questions")

## Groups
- Group leaderboard — ranked quiz scores within a group
- Group stats — most active member, highest scorer, most answered
- Group vs Group challenge mode

## Admin Additions
- Add custom survey questions specific to the event/organization
- Export data to CSV (user answers, quiz scores)
- Reset or archive a session (for recurring events)
- See a full quiz leaderboard across all users

## UI & Polish
- Celebratory animation on quiz correct answers (confetti, etc.)
- Dark mode
- Onboarding walkthrough for first-time users explaining how the app works
- Custom color themes per group/event
